Taco Salad Recipe

How to Make Taco Salad

This restaurant-worthy taco salad recipe is quick, easy, and full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle French dressing
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) package tortilla chips
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • Â½ cup prepared salsa
  • 4 tablespoons sour cream

Steps:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Drain and discard grease.
  4. Stir in chili beans, French-style dressing, and seasoning mix.
  5. Fill the empty dressing bottle 2/3 full of water and add to the skillet.
  6. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. Crush the bag of chips, open the bag, and toss crushed chips into a large bowl.
  8. Add lettuce, cheese, and tomatoes.
  9. Pour meat mixture into the bowl; mix well.
  10. Top with salsa and sour cream.
